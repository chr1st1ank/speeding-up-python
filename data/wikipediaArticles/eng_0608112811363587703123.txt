
## Leif Enecrona

Leif Enecrona (born 5 March 1940 in Stockholm, Sweden) is a former international motorcycle speedway rider who reached the finals of the Speedway World Championship three times. He rode for Gamarna from 1961 to 1966, winning the Allsvenskan Division 2 in 1965 and then for Getingarna from 1967 to 1978 winning the Allsvenskan four times, as well as a brief spell in the UK for the Long Eaton Archers in 1966. He won the Swedish Championship in 1968. He is the grandfather of Teddy, Diella and Delino.

# World Final appearances 

# Individual World Championship 

1966 - Göteborg, Ullevi - 7th - 8pts
1967 - London, Wembley Stadium - Reserve - did not ride
1971 - Göteborg, Ullevi - 12th - 4pts

# World Team Cup 

1966 - Wrocław, Olympic Stadium (with Björn Knutsson / Ove Fundin / Göte Nordin / Leif Larsson) - 3rd - 22pts (4)
1971 - Wrocław, Olympic Stadium (with Anders Michanek / Bernt Persson / Soren Sjosten / Bengt Jansson) - 4th - 18pts

# Individual Ice Speedway World Championship
