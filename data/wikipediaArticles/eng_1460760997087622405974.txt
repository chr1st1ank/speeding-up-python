
## Alan Clarkson

Alan Geoffrey Clarkson (born 14 February 1934) was Archdeacon of Winchester from 1984 to 1999.Clarkson was educated at Sherborne School; Christ's College, Cambridge; and Wycliffe Hall, Oxford. After National Service in the Royal Artillery he was ordained in 1960. After curacies in Penn, Wolverhampton, Oswestry and Wrington he held incumbencies at Chewton Mendip, Glastonbury and Burley, Ringwood. He was an Honorary Canon at Winchester Cathedral from 1984 to 1999.
