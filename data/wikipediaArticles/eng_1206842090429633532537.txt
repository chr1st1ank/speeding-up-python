
## Mischell Miljević-Sachpekidis

Mischell Miljević-Sachpekidis (Serbian Cyrillic: Мишел Миљевић-Схпекидис; born 30 October 1989) is a Swedish Serb football midfielder who plays for Radnik Surdulica in Serbian First League.
His father is Serb, and mother is Greek. His brother Kristijan is also footballer.

# Honours 

Radnik SurdulicaSerbian First League: 2014–15
