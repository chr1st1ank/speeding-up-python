
## Spurs W.F.C.

Spurs Women's Football Club is a South African women's association football club based in the Wynberg area of Cape Town.

# History 

This club was founded in 1986. The club has won numerous titles such as the Coke Cup and the National Vodacom Play off in 2003/2004 by beating Moroka Swallows Ladies, the defending champions. The team has been represented by players in the South African senior women's national team, at U-20.
