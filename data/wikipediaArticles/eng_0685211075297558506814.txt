
## My True Story

"My True Story" is a 1961 single recorded by The Jive Five. The single was the biggest hit for the group on both the R&B and pop charts. "My True Story" made it to number three on the Billboard Hot 100 and was number one on the R&B Sides chart for three weeks. It was the biggest hit for The Jive Five. Lead singer Eugene Pitt co-wrote the song.
